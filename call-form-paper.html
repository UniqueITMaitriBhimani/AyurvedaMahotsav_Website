Call for Papers

Paper and poster presentations have an important slot in the Seminar and participation with papers and posters on focal and sub-themes are invited.

Remember, you will be presenting to the researchers around the world! An opportunity that is rare and best.

  • Registration Required: Registration of one of the authors is mandatory for acceptance of abstract.
  • Abstracts should be submitted online through www.ayuryogexpo.com. Submission in any other form will not be accepted.
  • All submitted abstracts will be reviewed by the Academic Committee, which will decide the mode of presentation (oral or poster).
  • Authors will be notified of the acceptance by e-mail.
  • Oral presentations: 6 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for discussion.
  • Template for paper or poster presentation will be available on www.ayuryogexpo.com soon.
  • Posters should be submitted in PDF format.
